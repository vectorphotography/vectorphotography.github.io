Services

Wedding Photography & Videography

Wedding photography and videography are services that capture the memories of a couple's wedding day. Photography typically involves taking still images of the ceremony, reception, and other events throughout the day, while videography involves filming the events and creating a video that can be watched later. Both types of services can be provided by professional photographers and videographers who specialize in weddings, and many offer packages that include a combination of both photography and videography services.

Cinematic Pre/Post Wedding Video Shoot

A cinematic pre-wedding video shoot is a type of videography service that captures the story of a couple's relationship leading up to their wedding day. A post-wedding video shoot can be done after the wedding day to capture candid moments, speeches, and the overall atmosphere of the day. The final video can be edited to create a short film that highlights the most important moments of the day, with romantic music and motion graphics. Both pre and post wedding video shoot can be captured in a cinematic style, which typically involves the use of high-quality cameras and lenses, as well as professional lighting and sound equipment to create a polished, cinematic look and feel.

Biography & Documentary Shoot

A biography or documentary shoot is a type of videography service that captures the life story or a particular subject. A biography or documentary shoot typically involves extensive research and planning to ensure that the most important and interesting aspects of the subject are captured on film. This often involves conducting interviews with the subject and other people who are closely associated with them, as well as shooting footage of the subject in various locations and settings.

Areal Photography & Videography

Aerial photography and videography are types of services that capture images and footage from the air, typically using an aircraft such as a drone. Aerial photography involves taking still images from the air, while aerial videography involves filming footage.Aerial photography and videography can provide a unique perspective that is not possible to capture from the ground, and can be especially useful for capturing images of large areas such as cities, industrial sites, and natural landscapes. The use of drones in recent years has made aerial photography and videography more accessible and cost-effective.
